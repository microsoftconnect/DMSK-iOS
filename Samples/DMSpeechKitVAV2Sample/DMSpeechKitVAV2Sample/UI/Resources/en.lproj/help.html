Sample Phrases
Unless otherwise specified, say "Hey Dragon" or "OK Dragon" before the phrase.

Medication Orders
 * Order Tylenol 200 mg.
 * Order Tylenol 200 mg twice a day for 7 days.
 * Order 325 mg of Tylenol four times per day by mouth.
 
Reminders
 * Remind me to check Mr. Johnson’s dressing in four hours.
 * Remind me tomorrow to order more supplies.
 * Remind me to call Dr. Smith.
 * Remind me to message Doctor Adams on Friday.
 * Create a reminder at four P M to check on Baker.
 
Start a Note
  * Document admission note.
  * Dictate assessment.
 
While dictating ("Hey Dragon" is not needed in dictation)
  * Stop recording.
  * Stop listening.
 
Help (You get a different help if you say "What can I say" during dictation)

 * What can I say?
 * What will work with this?
