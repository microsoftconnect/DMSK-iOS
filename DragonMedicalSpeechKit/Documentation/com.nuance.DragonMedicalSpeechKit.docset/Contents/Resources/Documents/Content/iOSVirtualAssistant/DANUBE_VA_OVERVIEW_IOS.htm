Dragon Medical Virtual Assistant - overview

Dragon Medical Virtual Assistant (DMVA) supports a set of speech interactions between your app and your users. Our aim is to help physicians perform their daily tasks more quickly by using the easiest form of interaction possible - speech.

The DMVA dialog workflow assumes that your integration displays options and lists as part of the graphical user interface. DMVA isn't intended for voice-only user interfaces: Not all available options can be made available via voice prompts. Selecting from a list or seeing the results of a query is an important part of the interaction between your users and DMVA.

DMVA includes:

  • Searching charts, including lab test results.
  • Ordering medication.
  • Looking up patient data.
  • Communicating with other physicians and patients via speech and text.
  • Creating reminders.
  • Documenting sections and notes.
  • Showing schedule.
  • Selecting from a list.
  • Yes/no queries.
  • Text-to-speech.

Note: DMVA is only available in US Nuance-hosted systems and requires an additional license.
