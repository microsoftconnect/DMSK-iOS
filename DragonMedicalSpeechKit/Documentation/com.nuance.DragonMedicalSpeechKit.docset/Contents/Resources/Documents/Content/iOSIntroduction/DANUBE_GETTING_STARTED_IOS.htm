About Dragon Medical SpeechKit

Our aim is to help your users do their job more quickly by using the easiest form of interaction possible - speech. At the same time we understand the necessity of streamlining your development cycle. With a little preparation and a few easy steps, we can help you achieve this.

This is what you need

If you produce one of these, then integrating Dragon Medical SpeechKit is a breeze:

  • iPhone/iPad app
  • Browser-based EHR
  • .Net desktop app
  • COM desktop app
  • Android app
  • App with multiple text fields

This is what we can do for you

Here are the features available to you:

  • Speech to text conversion in real time
  • Voice controlled navigation
  • Correction via voice

Dragon Medical SpeechKit is designed so that, as we add additional features, you can upgrade to our most recent version safe in the knowledge that you don't have to plan integration changes into your development cycle.

This is what you don't need to worry about

Traditionally, integrating a third party product has certain drawbacks; with Dragon Medical SpeechKit there are no:

  • Steep learning curves
  • Long development cycles
  • Complex deployment projects
  • Late-night upgrade/maintenance windows

This is how simple it is

Administration tasks:

Register as a Nuance partner and order your licenses.

For more information, see our healthcare developer website.

Development tasks - a few hours:

Connect Dragon Medical SpeechKit to your application.

Finalization tasks - your normal process:

Test and release.

This is what you get

Your end result is a product that fulfills your internal requirements and that is enhanced with all our speech recognition features. The system is scalable and will support up-to-date technology.

For your users, it helps to speed up their work - it's easy to use and easy to navigate. To see the advantages for your users, see: End user documentation.
