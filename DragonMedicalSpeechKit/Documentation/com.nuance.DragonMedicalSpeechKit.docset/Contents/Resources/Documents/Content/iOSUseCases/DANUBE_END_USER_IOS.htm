End user online help

We provide HTML end user online help deployed with and hosted by the Dragon Medical Recognition Service. Our end user online help focuses on how your users can use speech recognition features. End users can access it via the Dragon Medical SpeechKit personalization & help screen.

Our end user online help is provided in the following languages:

  • Danish (da)
  • Dutch (nl)
  • English (en)
  • Finnish (fi)
  • French (fr)
  • German (de)
  • Italian (it)
  • Norwegian (no)
  • Portuguese (pt)
  • Spanish (es)
  • Swedish (sv)

Your app's end user online help

If your app's online help output is html-based, you can link it to the Dragon Medical SpeechKit personalization & help screen. The personalization & help screen can contain your online help or an index page that's branded for your app and that links to your online help.

Note: To display your app's end user online help in the personalization & help window, make sure it can be loaded in an iframe.

To connect your online help to the personalization & help screen, do the following:

  1. Set the NUSASession onlineHelpURL property to the URL where your online help is hosted.

    2. For example: [[NUSASession sharedSession] setOnlineHelpURL: @"https://your.server.com/OnlineHelp/index.html"]

  2. Initialize the speech recognition session.

When the personalization & help screen is opened your online help tab is available.

Note: Only HTTPS pages are supported.
